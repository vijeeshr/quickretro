Sprint retro app

Easily conduct a Sprint retrospective online with this free and open-source app

Features

  • No logins
  • Can have maximum 5 columns. Choose the column names you want.
  • Mask/Un-mask messages during the retro meeting (Board creator)
  • Update/Delete any message (Board creator)
  • Lock/Unlock board (Board creator)
  • Download as PDF (Board creator)
  • Online presence display
  • Auto-delete data in 2 hours

Add/Edit/Delete messages

Click buttons at the top of each column to add a message.
To edit a message, just click one of your existing messages. Press enter to save. Board creator update any message.
To delete, use the message's delete button.

Share url to participants

Use the Share button in left sidebar to get the board link to share to participants.

Customizing columns

Click the colored column bullets to enable or disable. Click the text to enter a separate column name other than the default.

Masking/Blocking/Downloading

Board creators can perform additional actions using buttons on the left sidebar.
Masking/UnMasking of messages.
Locking/Unlocking the board.
Downloaing messages as PDF.
